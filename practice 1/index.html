How to make protein shake

Ingredients

1. milk

2. Protein powder (flavor of your choice: vanilla, chocolate, etc.)

3. 1/2 frozen banana

4. 1 tablespoon of peanut butter (or any nut butter)

5. 1 teaspoon of honey or maple syrup (optional)


Optional Add-ins:

1. 1 tablespoon of chia seeds

2. 1/4 cup of Greek yogurt

3. A dash of cinnamon or cocoa powder
