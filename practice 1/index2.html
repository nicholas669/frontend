How to make Thanksgiving turkey

Ingredients

1. 1 whole turkey (10-14 lbs, adjust for your gathering size)

2. 1/2 cup unsalted butter (softened)

3. 2 tablespoons olive oil

4. 3 cloves garlic (minced)

5. 1 tablespoon fresh thyme leaves

6. 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary (chopped)

7. 1 tablespoon fresh sage (chopped)

8. Salt and black pepper (to taste)

9. 1 lemon (quartered)

10. 1 onion (quartered)

11. 2-3 cups chicken or turkey stock (for basting)

Optional Add-ins for Flavor:

1. 2-3 carrots (chopped)

2. 2-3 celery stalks (chopped)

3. A few sprigs of fresh herbs (thyme, rosemary, or sage) for stuffing the cavity.
